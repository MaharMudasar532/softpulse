## Curriculum Overview

### Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

SEO drives free organic traffic from Google. You learn keyword research, on-page optimization, meta tags, content strategy, internal linking, technical SEO basics, and local SEO for businesses in Sargodha and beyond. SEO skills compound over time — a well-ranked page brings leads for months without ad spend.

### Google Ads

Paid search puts businesses at the top of Google results immediately. You set up search campaigns, write ad copy, configure budgets, track conversions, and optimize cost-per-click. Local businesses — dentists, lawyers, tuition centres — depend on Google Ads for lead generation.

### Facebook and Instagram Advertising

Social platforms hold Pakistan's attention. You learn audience targeting, ad creative best practices, retargeting, lookalike audiences, campaign structures, and budget allocation. Instagram Reels and Stories ads require different thinking than feed posts — we cover both.

### Content Marketing

Content builds trust before the sale. You study blog strategy, video scripts, brand voice, storytelling frameworks, and content calendars. Good content supports SEO and reduces ad costs by warming audiences organically.

### Email Marketing

Email remains one of the highest-ROI channels. You learn list building, newsletter design, automation sequences, and segmentation. E-commerce brands and service businesses use email to recover abandoned carts and nurture leads.

### Analytics and Reporting

Data separates professionals from amateurs. Google Analytics, campaign dashboards, and client reporting teach you to measure what works, cut what fails, and present results clearly. Clients renew retainers when they see numbers — not promises.

## Career and Income Paths

### Freelance marketing retainers

Digital marketers in Pakistan manage Facebook ad accounts for local brands, run SEO for clinics and lawyers, and build content calendars for e-commerce stores. Monthly retainers of PKR 20,000–80,000 are standard for freelancers with proven campaign results. International clients on Upwork pay in dollars for similar work.

### Agency roles

Marketing agencies in Lahore, Karachi, and Islamabad hire SEO specialists, paid ads managers, and social media strategists. Junior roles start between PKR 40,000–80,000 monthly. Remote agency positions pay more.

### In-house marketing teams

Brands hire marketers to manage their online presence internally. E-commerce companies, ed-tech startups, and healthcare groups all need dedicated growth people.

### Business owners

The most direct ROI comes from applying skills to your own business. A restaurant owner who runs their own Instagram ads saves agency fees and understands their customer data intimately.

### Combining with design and e-commerce

Marketers who understand [graphic design](/blog/graphic-design-course-in-sargodha) produce better ad creatives without waiting for a designer. Marketers who understand [Shopify](/blog/shopify-course-in-sargodha) optimize product pages and ad funnels for e-commerce clients — a high-value combination on freelance platforms.
