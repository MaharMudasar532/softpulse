## Curriculum Overview

### Adobe Photoshop — Digital Graphics and Photo Editing

Photoshop is the industry standard for raster graphics. You learn layers, selections, masks, retouching, colour correction, banners, social media posts, and digital ad creatives. Every local business needs someone who can edit photos and create promotional graphics.

### Adobe Illustrator — Logos and Vector Branding

Logos must scale from business cards to billboards. Illustrator handles vector graphics — logos, icons, typography treatments, and brand assets that stay sharp at any size. You learn the full process from client brief to final file delivery.

### Canva — Speed and Client Deliverables

Not every project needs hours in Photoshop. Canva lets you produce professional social media templates, presentations, and quick client work fast. Freelancers use it daily for volume work and monthly retainers.

### Brand Identity Fundamentals

Software alone does not make a designer. You study colour theory, typography pairing, visual hierarchy, brand consistency, and the design thinking behind professional identity systems. Clients pay for judgment, not just tool knowledge.

### Social Media Design

Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube thumbnails each have different dimensions and audience expectations. You learn platform-specific design, content calendars, and how to maintain brand consistency across channels.

### Portfolio Development

Every assignment builds your portfolio. Behance, Instagram, and a personal website showcase your best work. We help you present projects professionally — mockups, case studies, and before/after comparisons that win client trust.

## Career and Income Paths

### Freelancing on Fiverr and Upwork

Graphic design has one of the shortest paths to first freelance income in Pakistan. A logo gig on Fiverr can earn $50–$300 from international clients. Social media post packages bring monthly retainers. Pakistani designers with strong portfolios and clear English communication consistently outperform local salary averages.

### Local business clients

Sargodha's retail shops, clinics, coaching centres, and restaurants need design work constantly. Word-of-mouth in local business networks generates steady projects — menus, shop banners, wedding cards, and event posters.

### Agency and in-house roles

Designers join marketing agencies, media houses, and brand teams. Junior designer roles in Pakistan start between PKR 35,000–70,000 monthly. Remote agency work pays more for designers with proven portfolios.

### Combining design with marketing

Designers who understand [digital marketing](/blog/digital-marketing-course-in-sargodha) offer complete packages — brand identity plus ad campaigns plus social strategy. That combination commands higher rates than design alone. Many Soft Pulse students take both courses for this reason.
